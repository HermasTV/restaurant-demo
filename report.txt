    Detect whether staff are wearing hairnets in mandated zones.
10- Unattended customer

11- Customer demographics
    Estimate aggregate age band / gender (privacy-preserving, no identity stored).

    Aggregated heat map showing where customers spend time.
13- Customer dwell time
    Per-zone time spent by customers (queue, table, counter).
